Welcome to Hogwards

Who is Harry Potter?

Harry Potter is a young wizard who survived the Dark Lord Voldemort's curse and grew up to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Along with his friends Ron and Hermione, he battles dark forces and discovers his true destiny.

Meet the Professors

Albus Dumbledore

Headmaster of Hogwarts. Wise, powerful, and kind-hearted leader.

Severus Snape

Potions Master. Dark past, but a true hero in disguise.

Minerva McGonagall

Transfiguration expert. Fierce, loyal, and highly respected.

Remus Lupin

Defense Against the Dark Arts teacher. Calm and caring werewolf.

Rubeus Hagrid

Keeper of Keys and Grounds. Loves magical creatures and Harry.

Sybill Trelawney

Divination professor. Eccentric but made real prophecies.

Filius Flitwick

Charms Master. Small in size, big in magical skill.

Alastor Moody

Legendary Auror. "Constant vigilance!" even when he's not himself.

Harry’s Friends

Hermione Granger

Brightest witch of her age, always by Harry’s side.

Ron Weasley

Harry’s loyal best friend and a brave Gryffindor.

Neville Longbottom

Once timid, now a true hero in the fight against evil.

Luna Lovegood

Eccentric and wise, Luna is always kind and brave.

Harry’s Enemies

Lord Voldemort

The Dark Lord, feared by all. Obsessed with power and immortality.

Bellatrix Lestrange

One of Voldemort’s most devoted followers. Completely unhinged.

Draco Malfoy

Harry's school rival, struggling between fear and pride.

Dolores Umbridge

Cruel, manipulative, and obsessed with control. Almost worse than Voldemort.

And so, the story goes on...

From enchanted classrooms to battles with darkness,
the magic never truly ends at Hogwarts.
